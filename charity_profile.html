Meals on Wheels






When: During lunch and dinner hours

Where: Multiple locations (See what's closest to you!)

Dates: Always

Skills: N/A

America, Let's Do Lunch!

Deliver a warm meal and make someone's day. 10 million seniors in America face the threat of hunger. Millions more live alone in isolation.

Learn more about what it takes to volunteer:
This involves picking up prepackaged meals from a central location and delivering them to the homes of senior citizens on a pedetermined route in your area. This will take roughly one hour.
